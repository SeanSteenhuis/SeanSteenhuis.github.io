Background

Electrical Apprentice

During my Electrical Apprentice I gained experience in Industrial, Residential, and Commercial settings. I would help the Journey men with any task giving to me. My daily task's consisted of cleaning the shop, packing the trucks for the journey men and assisting with jobs like installing Light Ballasts, Receptacles, Chandeliers, Solar Panels or helping to bend conduit, and digging trenches for running conduit. I work with a high degree of ownership, accountability, and integrity and would be a asset to any company.

Electrical Engineering Technologies

As A Graduate From The Electrical Engineer Technology Program at Conestoga College I have gained experience with electric motors, PLCs (Allen-Bradley & Siemens), generators, transformers and their industrial applications. I am constantly striving to learn more and improve in my field.
